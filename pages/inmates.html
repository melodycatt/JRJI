Preparing Inmates


Preparing Inmates for Reintegration
To make a difference to those suffering inside correctional facilities, JRJI will provide training and education for prison staff, teaching them how to foster change in inmates rather than trying to inflict punishment. We will also lead inmate-led programs about self-help and trying to restore yourself, as well as offering post-release support for reintegration into society. This will directly help those who the organisation is focused on, and allow prisoners to take direct action in trying to change for the better, leading to healing both inside and outside of prison.
